About Me

Dancer & Dance Teacher

For 11 years I was involved in rhythmic and free-acrobatic gymnastics. At the age of 14 I was occupied with in Latin dances as well as other types of dances. At the age of 16 I decided to study Latin professionally, I started taking classes to get the degrees I needed. With the help of well-known and trained teachers such as Sandra Gavala and Maria Apospori in Latin International and Sandra Gavala and Marianna Karamanakou in Latin American I was able to learn correctly and well what they needed.
I have managed to obtain the following diplomas:

  • Bronze 1 in Latin American.
      1. Dances: Rumba, Cha-Cha, Samba, Swing, Mambo, Waltz, Foxtrot, Tango.
      2. Degree: Distinction
